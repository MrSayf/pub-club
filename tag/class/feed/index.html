Placentia, CA – Pub Club Leads is proud to announce that the American Marketing Association, in connection with California State University, will be visiting their facilities on Friday, February 23rd, 2018 at 3:30 PM PST.

As a leader in the world of online marketing, Pub Club Leads is eager to show students what the marketing life is like on the inside, give them a tour of the facilities, and even discuss potential internship opportunities.

Pub Club Leads was founded in 2008 by Adam Sthay to help online businesses thrive by developing successful strategies, including email marketing, PPC, digital marketing, lead generation, and much more.
